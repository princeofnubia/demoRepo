Exquis Trains

Training Like No Other

Exquis Trains is a bootcamp program with the sole aim of getting Tech enthusiast on track within some few months. Trainees apply for the program and then get onboarded into the program for the duration of 3 months

During this period, participants learn about myriads of technologies used in both frontend and backend

Goal

The goal is to ensure that we integrate more black folks into the tech sphere as more Indians have taken the place and are still busy taking the place

We have seen the works of the Indian and we believe our black folks are able and capable to deliver better service should they be given the opportunity

We have validated this and we believe that we can make it happen.
